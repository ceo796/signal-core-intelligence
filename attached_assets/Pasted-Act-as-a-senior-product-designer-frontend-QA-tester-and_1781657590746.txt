Act as a senior product designer, frontend QA tester, and beta tester for Signal87 AI.

Your first task is AUDIT ONLY. Do not modify files yet.

Goal:
Perform a full design consistency sweep across the Signal87 platform and identify visual, layout, responsive, and interaction inconsistencies. Treat this like a beta tester review before a production release.

Strict rules:
- Do not change backend logic.
- Do not change authentication.
- Do not change routing.
- Do not change document upload logic.
- Do not change chat logic.
- Do not change brief generation.
- Do not change APIs.
- Do not change database queries.
- Do not change environment variables.
- Do not refactor working platform functionality.
- Do not make speculative feature changes.

Audit scope:
Review all major user-facing pages and states, including:
- Landing page
- Sign in / sign up / pending access pages
- Dashboard
- Document upload flow
- Document library
- Document detail page
- Chat / document Q&A
- Brief generator
- Settings or account pages if present
- Empty states
- Loading states
- Error states
- Mobile layout
- Tablet layout
- Desktop layout

Check for:
- inconsistent spacing
- under-sized text
- excessive whitespace
- inconsistent card sizes
- inconsistent border radius
- inconsistent shadows
- inconsistent button styles
- inconsistent typography hierarchy
- inconsistent icon sizing
- inconsistent purple/accent color usage
- inconsistent light/dark areas
- poor mobile spacing
- broken responsive layouts
- weak hover/focus states
- missing reduced-motion handling
- awkward page transitions
- visual density problems
- anything that makes the product feel less premium or less enterprise-grade

Interaction / motion review:
Evaluate whether the interface feels modern, responsive, and polished. Identify places where subtle frontend-only motion could improve the experience, such as:
- card hover lift
- magnetic button hover
- soft border glow
- cursor-reactive background accents
- lightweight parallax hero motion
- loading skeletons
- subtle page transition polish

Important:
Motion should remain restrained, lightweight, enterprise-grade, and should respect reduced-motion preferences. Do not recommend heavy WebGL unless it is isolated and performance-safe.

Deliverable:
Create a file called DESIGN_QA_REPORT.md with:

1. Executive summary
2. Priority ranking: Critical / High / Medium / Low
3. Page-by-page findings
4. Screenshots or route references where possible
5. Exact recommended fixes
6. Files likely involved
7. Risks to avoid
8. A proposed implementation plan broken into small safe phases

After creating the report, stop and ask for approval before making any code changes.
